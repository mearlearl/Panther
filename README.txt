Background:  

This is a fun little framework I made during the initial quarantine of May 2020 to do some 3D plotting.
It is super adaptable and I have used it for lots of small projects including plotting 3D surfaces, demonstrating deformation tensors, plotting 3D vector fields, looking at STL files, etc.
